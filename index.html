Welcome to Pura Vida Restaurant
At Pura Vida Restaurant, we bring the authentic flavors of Costa Rica to your table with love and tradition. Our business, led by two passionate women and one dedicated man, offers a delicious variety of typical Costa Rican dishes, homemade desserts, and healthy/fitness meals prepared with fresh, local ingredients.
Whether you're craving a warm plate of gallo pinto, a refreshing ceviche, or a guilt-free quinoa bowl, we’ve got something for everyone. And for your special events, we provide professional catering services tailored to your needs perfect for birthdays, meetings, and celebrations. Let us share the Pura Vida spirit through great food and friendly service. Your taste of Costa Rica starts here!
